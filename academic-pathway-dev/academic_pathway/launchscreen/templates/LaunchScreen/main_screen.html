Interests themselves do not need to be stored in the DB

We can store the values for interests within the forms that we create to filter for specific majors and classes. For example, if an individual would fill out the form like so:
Starting Salary: $80,000+
Primary Interest: Technology
Secondary Interest 1: Critical Thinking
Secondary Interest 2: Hands-on

This person would receive a recommendation to acquire the Information Systems major. They would also be advised to take on the class IS436, as they filled in 'hands-on' in their interest requirements. We will need to work out a system for determining major 'difficulty', right now in the DB it's a numerical scale, maybe EASY MEDIUM HARD would be better. Maybe we shouldn't even open the can of worms that is comparing difficulty of majors though. I certainly don't want to ruffle any feathers.
